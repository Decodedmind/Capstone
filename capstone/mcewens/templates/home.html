Our Story

Discover the essence of Mcewen's Oxford, a realm of fervor for fine dining, exquisite wines, and an unwavering commitment to your satisfaction. Our sanctuary is designed to beckon you back, becoming a cherished destination with an eclectic blend of design influences. At the heart of Mcewen's Oxford lies a passion for elevating Southern cuisine to unparalleled heights. Lunch unveils a tapestry of delectable options including superb sandwiches, vibrant salads, and distinguished business-centric entrées. As the sun sets, dinner unfolds as a canvas for culinary creativity and sophistication. Our dedicated staff stands ready to orchestrate your experience, ensuring every moment is memorable. In addition to our inviting bar and dining sanctuary, an exclusive private room awaits, tailored for gatherings of up to 25 individuals. Embrace the allure of Mcewen's Oxford, where the art of gastronomy, the allure of wine, and your desires intertwine harmoniously, inviting you to return time and again.
