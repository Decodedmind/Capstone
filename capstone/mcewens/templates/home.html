Our Story

Welcome to McEwen's, a culinary landmark nestled in the heart of Oxford, Mississippi. Our story began with Chef James McEwen's vision to combine his southern heritage with innovative flavors, resulting in a dining experience that has captivated locals and visitors alike. From the moment you step through our doors, you'll be enveloped in the tantalizing aroma of expertly crafted dishes showcasing locally sourced ingredients. Our menu pays homage to Mississippi's culinary traditions, featuring delights such as crispy catfish, succulent smoked pork ribs, and mouthwatering pecan pie. Beyond the exceptional food, McEwen's embraces the spirit of community, supporting local farmers and nurturing aspiring chefs through our culinary scholarship program. As we continue to create new flavors, forge friendships, and make lasting memories, McEwen's remains a cherished destination where the power of good food brings people together. Join us on this culinary journey and experience the magic of McEwen's.
